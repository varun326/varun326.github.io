Political history club

Our Goal

Our goal is to lead a club that has topics and knowledge introduced by YOU. It is up to you to choose what you want to debate and present. This way we ensure that we learn what we want to and gain new interesting knowledge. We accept any perspective and ideals. We make sure that the club is very student centered.

Our objective and what we plan to do for this club

To develop a nonpartisan platform for meaningfully enriching academic activity, with a specific interest in political science, philosophy, and history. Through our engagement in balanced dialogue, we provide a variety of ways for you to exchange ideas. Our mission is to unite the politically active students to promote an engaging, respectful discourse and activism regardless of partisan affiliation
