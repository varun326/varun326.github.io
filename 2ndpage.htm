Political history club

Activities you will be doing in this club

There will be three types of activities we will be indulging onto which include:

  • Case studies with student-led presentations
  • Historical crisis simulations with debates and contextual learning
  • High School Political Review which is a platform for students to express the knowledge they teach and learn + Express opinions about political topics and current affairs

    • The topics that you are going to discuss and research about include:

    Kashmir Hong Kong Protests Nanking Massacre
    Tiananmen Square Romanov Family Religious Violence
    Islamic Jihad Northwest China History of RSS
    Sengoku Jidai Hiroshima & Nagasaki The influence of Gandhi
    Rwandan Genocide Dalits (social inequality in India)
