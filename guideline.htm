Political history club

Guidelines to follow

Whenever you are given a topic, there are some key points that you need to answer for example:
  • Some background about your topic
  • Important political figures
  • Timeline of your research case study
  • The causes of the topic
  • Consequences of the topic

    • Instruction to follow

    1. The information you present must be supported
    2. You must share your views and perspectives
    3. Any argument you present must have a solid foundation
    4. Discuss alternate views and possible interpretations
    5. Make it visual
